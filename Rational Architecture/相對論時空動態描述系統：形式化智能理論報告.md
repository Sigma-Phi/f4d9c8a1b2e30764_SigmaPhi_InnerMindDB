## English Version (~300 words)

This theory reformulates spacetime as a stochastic, partially observable controlled dynamical system. The underlying state $X_t$ represents a latent geometric-physical structure (analogous to a world model), while observations $O_t$ are noisy, frame-dependent projections of that hidden reality. An AI agent interacts with this system through control inputs $U_t$, which influence the evolution of the latent state via nonlinear stochastic dynamics.

From an AI systems perspective, this constructs a deep analogy between spacetime physics and agentic learning: the metric tensor corresponds to the internal representation of environment structure, while control signals correspond to policy actions. The learning objective is not merely prediction, but structural alignment between inferred dynamics and true generative processes.

The system is governed by a fundamental tension between two forces: (1) information maximization, which encourages the agent to reduce uncertainty and align representations across observation frames; and (2) energy or control cost minimization, which penalizes excessive intervention or unstable policy updates. This creates a critical trade-off analogous to variational inference under dynamical constraints.

At the critical regime, the system achieves optimal information efficiency: representations become maximally predictive yet minimally complex, and causal consistency emerges across trajectories. This corresponds to a phase where effective dimensionality stabilizes, analogous to a learned 4D spacetime manifold.

Outside this regime, three behaviors emerge: over-exploration (chaotic latent dynamics), critical learning (optimal generalization), and over-constrained collapse (degenerate representations or overfitting). In modern AI terms, this maps directly to the balance between exploration, generalization, and model collapse in large-scale agentic systems.

## 1. 形式系統生成（Formal System Construction）

### 中文
定義系統狀態、觀測、控制與隨機動力學：

$$X_t \in \mathbb{R}^d$$

$$O_t = h(X_t) + \varepsilon_t,  \quad \varepsilon_t \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \sigma^2I)$$

$$U_t \in \mathbb{R}^k$$

$$dX_t = F(X_t, O_t, U_t)dt + G(X_t, O_t, U_t)dW_t$$

在時空動態系統中：
* $X_t$ 代表四維時空度規張量 $g_{\mu\nu}$ 與物質場的聯合狀態向量。
* $O_t$ 代表觀測者在特定參考系下測得的局部物理量（如局部流速、觀測固有時）。
* $U_t$ 代表非慣性系下的坐標變換控制或外部能量注入。
* $dW_t$ 代表量子漲落或背景引力波噪聲引發的微觀時空微擾。

### English
Define a stochastic controlled dynamical system with noisy observations and diffusion dynamics. In this system, $X_t$ formalizes the joint configuration of the spacetime metric tensor $g_{\mu\nu}$ and the stress-energy distribution, while $O_t$ captures frame-dependent local measurements under quantum-gravitational or thermal fluctuations $dW_t$.
