# Δ→Δ′ 自回寫理論

---

## 0. 大白話理論介紹（Plain-language + AI Application View）

### 中文

這個理論只有一個核心機制：系統不是在「解決問題」，而是在「改寫問題本身」。

傳統 AI 或學習系統通常是這樣運作的：看到誤差 → 修正參數 → 讓輸出更接近目標。但在 Δ→Δ′ 自回寫理論裡，誤差（Δ）不只是被修正，它會被轉換成新的誤差形式（Δ′），然後寫回系統內部，成為下一輪運作的一部分。

也就是說，系統不是在收斂，而是在持續改寫「什麼叫做誤差」。

因此學習不再是走向答案，而是持續重構問題空間本身。每一次更新，都不是消除差距，而是把差距變成另一種差距。

在 AI 角度，它代表一種極端的 meta-learning 形式：不只模型在學，連「學習規則」也被學習；不只策略在變，連「什麼是策略更新」也在變。

最關鍵的結果是：系統沒有終點狀態，只有持續自我改寫的動態流。

---

### English

This theory proposes a single core mechanism: the system does not solve problems—it rewrites what counts as a problem.

In conventional learning systems, an error (Δ) is used to update parameters so that future outputs better match a target. In the Δ→Δ′ Self-Rewriting framework, however, the discrepancy is not merely corrected. It is transformed into a new discrepancy Δ′, which is then fed back into the system as part of its own structure.

Thus, learning is not convergence toward a solution but continuous reconstruction of the problem space itself.

From an AI perspective, this corresponds to extreme meta-learning: not only are model parameters updated, but the very rule of updating is also subject to change.

The system therefore has no final equilibrium state—only an ongoing process of self-rewriting dynamics.

## 1. 形式系統生成（Formal System Construction）

### 中文

定義內部差異：

\[
\Delta_t \in \mathbb{R}^n
\]

系統不直接消除 Δ，而是：

\[
\Delta_t \rightarrow \Delta'_t
\]

並回寫系統結構：

\[
X_{t+1} = X_t + \mathcal{R}(\Delta'_t)
\]

其中回寫算子：

\[
\mathcal{R}_{t+1} = \Phi(\mathcal{R}_t, \Delta_t)
\]

### English

A system where discrepancies are transformed rather than resolved, and the transformation rule itself is recursively updated.

## 3. 動態方程（Dynamics Equation）

### 中文

\[
\Delta_{t+1} = \Psi(\Delta_t, \mathcal{R}_t)
\]
\[
\mathcal{R}_{t+1} = \mathcal{R}_t + \alpha \cdot \Phi(\Delta_t)
\]

核心耦合：

\[
\Delta_t \rightarrow \Delta'_t = f(\Delta_t, \mathcal{R}_t)
\]

### English

Discrepancies and rewrite rules co-evolve in a coupled recursive loop.

## 4. 相變結構（Phase Structure）

| Phase | Condition | Behavior |
|------|----------|----------|
| Frozen | \(R_t \rightarrow 0\) | no rewriting, classical learning |
| Critical | \(R_t \approx R_c\) | stable self-rewriting |
| Chaotic | \(R_t \rightarrow high\) | loss of structure, Δ explosion |

---

## 5. 主定理（Main Theorem）

### 中文

存在臨界自回寫率 \(R_c\)，使得：

\[
R_t \rightarrow R_c \Rightarrow \max I(\Delta_t ; \mathcal{R}_t)
\]

系統達到最大學習效率與最大結構可塑性共存。

### English

There exists a critical rewrite rate where mutual information between discrepancy and rewrite rule is maximized.

---

## 6. Lyapunov 穩定性（Stability）

### 中文

傳統收斂不成立，改為：

\[
V(\mathcal{R}_t) = \mathrm{distance\ to\ identity\ manifold}
\]

\[
\frac{dV}{dt} \approx \text{bounded oscillation under continuous rewriting}
\]

### English

Stability is defined not as convergence, but as bounded persistence of identity under continuous self-modification.

Empirically test systems where both optimization targets and optimization rules are learnable.

### English
